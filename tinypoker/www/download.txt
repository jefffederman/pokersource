Download
========

libtinypoker
------------

*libtinypoker* is a small client/server library that implements the
Internet Poker Protocol (IPP) 2.0 over TLS. It is licensed under the
terms of the http://tinypoker.org/gpl-3.html[GNU General Public License version 3 or Later].
The only dependency is http://www.gnu.org/software/gnutls/[GNU TLS].

 * 'No releases yet'.

tinypokerd
----------

*tinypokerd* is a small poker server. It is licensed under the 
terms of the http://tinypoker.org/gpl-3.html[GNU General Public License version 3 or Later].
It depends on http://tinypoker.org/[libtinypoker], http://www.nongnu.org/confuse/[libConfuse],
http://0pointer.de/lennart/projects/libdaemon/[libdaemon] and
http://sqlite.org/[SQLite].

 * 'No releases yet'.

bagelbot-ng
-----------

*bagelbot-ng* is a trivial client for *tinypokerd*. It will connect to a
server, join a game, and fold every hand. It is intended
to be a base for building a poker bot. It is licensed under the
terms of the http://tinypoker.org/gpl-3.html[GNU General Public License version 3 or Later].
It depends on http://tinypoker.org/[libtinypoker].

 * 'No releases yet'.
